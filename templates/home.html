How it works

01
Add the bot and run /setup

Invite RoLock to your server and run /setup. The bot immediately sweeps all current members, banning anyone already on the list.

02
Bans happen on join and every 5 minutes

When a blacklisted player joins your server, they're banned instantly. Every 5 minutes, the bot checks for new banlist entries and sweeps all protected servers.

03
Configure your protection level

Use /configure to choose your enforcement tier: LL3 only, LL2+3 (default), or all entries. Use /whitelist_add to exempt specific users from bans in your server.

How the banlist is decided

01
Anyone can submit a report

Reports are submitted through the RoLock bot using /report, with supporting evidence - screenshots, logs, video clips, or other documentation.

02
RoLock staff screen the report

Staff review whether the report is valid and meets minimum requirements - sufficient evidence, a real offense, a real account. Reports that don't clear this bar are rejected here and go no further.

03
The entire RoLock community votes

Approved reports are put to a community vote. Every member of the RoLock Discord receives a ballot with the full case details and compiled evidence. A 75% majority is required to pass - votes are recorded with optional written reasons for accountability.

04
Player is added with a lock level

If the vote passes, the player is added to the banlist. Their lock level is assigned based on the severity of what they did. All protected servers are updated automatically within minutes.

Lock levels

LL3 Critical

Protection from people who are actively attempting to attack or raid servers. Enforced by default.

LL2 Serious

Protection from people who have targeted multiple individuals - whether sexually or through deliberate doxxing - to cause harm. Enforced by default.

LL1 Moderate

Protection from people who have repeatedly shown intolerant views - excessive harassment, discrimination, racism, transphobia, etc. Not enforced by default; server admins opt in with /configure.

Rotector Integration

01
A separate database of known predators

Rotector is a third-party database that tracks Roblox accounts tied to condo servers and flags known groomers and pedophiles.

02
Automatic bans on join, no overlap required

When Rotector is enabled, RoLock checks every joining member against Rotector's database. Anyone flagged is banned instantly, even if they are not on the RoLock banlist.

03
Enable with /rotector_toggle

Rotector is off by default. Run /rotector_toggle in your server to turn it on. The bot handles all lookups automatically on every join from that point forward.
